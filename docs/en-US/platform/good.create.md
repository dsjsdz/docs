# 5.3 Add / Edit

![2025-11-23_085533.jpg](/images/2025-11-24_161029.jpg){style="background: #fff; padding: 1rem; border-radius: 0.5rem;"}

## 1. Product Name

> The product name displayed in the app, mini-program, or H5 product page. Special characters, such as /, are not allowed.

## 2. Product Content

> A rich-text description of the product, supporting HTML format, including images, videos, etc.

## 3. Product Description

> A short description of the product. Special characters are not allowed. When displayed in a card view, it appears below the product name.

## 4. Product Attributes

> The attributes of the product. Multiple attribute values are supported.

## 5. Product Code

> The unique identifier of the product. Special characters are not allowed. Typically corresponds to the product barcode (EAN-13).

## 6. Price

> The selling price of the product, supporting up to two decimal places.

Membership price: supported after v1.30.0. Current version: v1.24.0.

## 7. Cover Image

> The product cover image, supporting JPG, JPEG, and PNG formats. Recommended size: 400x400px. Multiple images are supported, up to 5 images.

## 8. Tax-Free Product

> Indicates whether the product is tax-free. Non-tax-free products must have a tax rate set.
