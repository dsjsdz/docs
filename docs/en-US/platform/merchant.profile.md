# ️ 2.3 Merchant Information

Merchant information can be obtained whether you create a merchant or join a merchant.

![merchant](/images/2025-11-24_154329.jpg){style="background: #fff; padding: 1rem; border-radius: 0.5rem;"}

All vending machines under the merchant can be assigned to other administrators.

## Edit Merchant

![merchant](/images/2025-11-24_154400.jpg){style="background: #fff; padding: 1rem; border-radius: 0.5rem;"}

Approved merchants can modify their merchant name, merchant description, and merchant logo.

> Once a merchant is approved, it cannot be deleted. You may apply to change the administrator.
