# 2.7 Change Administrator

Click Staff Management → Administrator Panel → Change Administrator to display the guided workflow popup.

![2025-11-23_085533.jpg](/images/2025-11-24_155656.jpg){style="background: #fff; padding: 1rem; border-radius: 0.5rem;"}

⚠️ The merchant administrator can only be transferred to a regular merchant member (a member who has passed the review).

![2025-11-23_085533.jpg](/images/2025-11-24_155713.jpg){style="background: #fff; padding: 1rem; border-radius: 0.5rem;"}

⚠️ The current administrator will remain in the merchant, and the new owner will be granted operational permissions.
